Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a wide range of services, including full-stack web development, graphic design, and video editing. From creating responsive websites to producing high-quality visuals and video content, I tailor my services to meet your specific needs.

What technologies do you work with?

I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, React.js, and Node.js for web development. For graphic design and video editing, I use Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro.

Can you help with branding and logo design?

Yes, I specialize in graphic design services, which include creating unique and memorable logos and brand identities that resonate with your audience.

Do you offer video editing services for social media content?

Yes, I provide video editing services tailored for social media platforms such as Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook, ensuring your content is engaging and optimized for each platform's requirements.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The time required to complete a project depends on its complexity and scope. Simple projects like logo designs may take a few days, while more complex projects like website development can take a few weeks. I will always provide an estimated timeline after discussing the project details.

What are your payment terms?

I usually require a 50% deposit upfront, with the remainder due upon project completion. However, I'm flexible and willing to discuss payment terms based on the project's size and scope.

Do you provide ongoing support after the project is completed?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance services for websites and other projects. This ensures everything continues to function smoothly and any necessary updates are handled promptly.

How can I get started with a project?

You can contact me through my website’s contact form or send me an email. We will discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget, and I will provide a detailed proposal.
