Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by the websites you visit. I use cookies to help improve the performance and functionality of this website.

2. How I Use Cookies

I use cookies to improve your experience on this site, track user activity, and personalize content for you. These cookies do not personally identify you but help provide a more personalized experience.

3. Types of Cookies I Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help me understand how visitors interact with my website and improve its performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences to enhance your experience (e.g., language settings).
  • Advertising Cookies: These track your activity to display relevant advertisements.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage and delete cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website. For more information, you can refer to this guide on managing cookies.

5. Changes to My Cookie Policy

I may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, with the date of the update indicated at the top.
